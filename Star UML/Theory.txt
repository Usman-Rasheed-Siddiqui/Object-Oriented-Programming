Aggregation: 
-"has a" relationship
- Unidirectional
- If independent is destroyed, dependent also gets destroyed.
	--For e.g, if wheels are destroyed, car also has no use left.

Composition:
- "part of"relationship
- Bidirectional
- If anyone is destroyed, the other will also be destroyed.
	-- If a person's heart or the person is destroyed, the other will not survive. 

Association:
- Does not use the associated class objects
